About Me

My name is Richard. I'm a T-shaped person, meaning my model hasn't been animated properly yet I have a broad range of skills but specialise in dynamic heavy metal music for video games using FMOD Studio. Give your game the headbanging, blood pumping love it deserves - shoot me an email on the contact page and we'll get this mosh pit started!

Main skills: FMOD Studio (scripting, integration, dynamic music), heavy metal, rock, punk, 80s rock ballad, whatever the hell that rock style was in 90's PC Engine shmups

Other skills: Japanese (basic conversation), puns, Reaper, C# (Unity), Python, Ruby, voice over (tutorial and character), creative swearing, insisting you cannot measure butter by cups

Boring gear stuff nerds care about: Haze fan fret 7 string guitar, Fender MIJ Stratocaster, Ibanez S series something or other to go widdly woo on, Bias FX 2, Reaper, copious amounts of black coffee
