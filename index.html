Services

Hastega Consultancy provides a wide range of services for game studios of all shapes and sizes.

Agile Training

  • Custom Agile Workshops

    Interactive sessions on Scrum, Kanban, SAFe, XP, and Lean tailored to you and your teams.


  • Role-Based Training

    Practical learning for POs, Scrum Masters, delivery leads, and cross-functional teams.


  • Agile Fundamentals & Onboarding

    Get new teams Agile-ready with structured, engaging foundational training.

Agile Delivery Support

  • Agile Transformation Strategy

    End-to-end guidance for scaling agility across teams, departments, or the entire organisation.


  • Team Coaching & Mentoring

    Embedded coaching for Scrum Masters, Product Owners, developers, QA, and leadership.


  • Delivery Health Checks

    Assess team maturity, velocity, blockers, and alignment to Agile best practices.

Agile Process Implementation

  • Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) Consulting

    Navigate the complexities of enterprise agility with experienced SAFe practitioners.


  • Change Management & Culture Shift

    Build lasting agility through mindset coaching, leadership support, and cultural evolution.


  • Tooling & Workflow Setup

    Optimise tools like Jira, Confluence, and Azure DevOps to support Agile at scale.

Advantages

What sets Hastega Consultancy apart from the rest?

Global

Hastega Consultancy works around your schedule and time zone. Video call or emails only, whatever suits your wants and needs.

Game Dev Specialists

Corporate level Agile likely won't suit the fast paced real-time nature of video games. Hastega Consultancy has deep experience in video game development big and small, providing you with practical advice to get the best out of your next release.

Not Just Processes, Not Just Games

From sound design to QA to management, Hastega Consultancy has everything you need for your game studio or startup.
